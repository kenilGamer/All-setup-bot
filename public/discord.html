Godcraft Discord Bot Terms of Service

Welcome to GODCRAFT Discord Bot! We take our server very seriously and with that, we need a good set of rules to go along with it. Please keep in mind that not all of these rules are 100% absolute. Meaning that the context of a situation may change the outcome/punishment. Make sure you take the time to read over this page and check back up on it every now and then when we make changes to it. Please also keep in mind that just because a rule is not written does not mean it is not punishable. There are some things that are just common sense and should be kept that way!

1. You may not talk about hacking/cheating (in games) in any way shape or form. This includes asking for help with the making of illegal software.
2. You are allowed to curse moderately. Excessive cursing and/or cursing towards other members is not allowed.
3. You can only share links from a reputable source. Distributing shortened links and/or links that automatically start downloads is not allowed.
4. Advertising on the server, in private message with members of the server, and/or in your status is not allowed. This includes promoting other servers, channels, websites and more.
5. You are not allowed to spam on the server. This includes farming experience/levels.
6. You may not unnecessarily tag high staff members, this includes development help. When tagging a staff member include the message in it.
7. You may not impersonate staff members.
8. You may not disrespect staff. This includes swearing at staff members.
9. You are not allowed to share any NSFW media/content. This includes sending links, images, GIFs, and videos.
10. You should not bash other members and/or start/participate in heated arguments.
11. One should always follow staffs orders. If one does not follow one's orders punishments can be made.
12. You should only speak english, non encrypted english, as that is our servers primary language.
13. Do not be racist, sexist, or toxic towards other users.
14. Alterative Accounts are not allowed on this server and will be kicked.
15. Please do not send ear rape files, or ear rape people in any of our voice chats.
16. Any commentsrelated to hacking, cheating, or breaking the games TOS is NOT ALLOWED.
17. Breaking or glitching any products of Hypertonic Discord Bot in any way is NOT ALLOWED and will result in immediate action!
18. Mass using spoilers, or using lengthy multilined spoilers, is strictly prohibited! Spoilers are allowed but are to be kept at an absolute minimum.
19. Do not overuse clutter characters/unicode in usernames/nicknames.
20. Breaking Discord TOS will result in punishment.
21. Spamming is seen as sending multiple messages with the same content after each other or sending messages without content that contributes to the conversation that is currently ongoing and/or a new conversation.
22. Acting as if you have a staff position, changing your name to one of a staff member, or choosing the same profile picture is seen as impersonation.
23. NSFW (Not Safe For Work) is seen as anything that includes mild to hard nudity, a sexual underlying meaning, racial slurs and degrading content. If one is offended by the media/content shared, they can point this out to staff and punishments can be made.
24. The same rules still apply to staff members. If any staff is seen breaking the rules please report this to a higher staff member.
25. You should include relevant code and/or an error in the help message, doing so can improve the speed of getting the issue solved.
26. You may not post more than 20 lines of code/error in a development channel.
27. You can not tag/dm staff members who do not have the code magician role for development help. People who are willing to help will check the development channels regularly and will help when they can.
28. Before asking for help, please ensure that you know the fundamentals of the base language(s) you are coding in!
29. You may not have general conversations in any of the community channels. If you want to contact somebody you must do this through general chat and/or private messages.
30. You may only share links from a reputable source. Distributing shortened links and/or links that automatically start downloads is not allowed.
31. You are not allowed to re-upload projects you shared before. This includes updates, patches, fixes.
32. You must always follow the format pinned in the channel. Failing to follow the format will result in your message being removed.
